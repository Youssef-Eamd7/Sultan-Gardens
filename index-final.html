Sultan Gardens Resort

The Perfect All Inclusive Resort in Sharm El Sheikh

Welcome to Sultan Gardens Resort - One of the finest all-around resorts on the picturesque Red Sea; The multiple Award-winning, certified Eco-Friendly and strategically located in Shark's Bay between Sharm El-Sheikh International Airport and the city centre, the resort boasts 220 meters of private sandy beaches, a breadth of beautiful coral formations with an unrivalled view of the stunning Tiran Island.

Embrace upscale comfort in 520 spacious rooms and suites surrounded by thriving gardens, picturesque scenery along with a wide range of facilities and amenities and Contemporary fine dining options including six restaurants. Guests will also enjoy food served at one of our multiple pools and beach bars or may choose to take advantage of 24-hour room service offering a robust and exciting menu selection.
